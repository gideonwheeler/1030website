Hippo Fun Facts

  • There used to be many species of hippo, but modernly there are only two who have survived: Common hippos, and pygmy hippos (see image below).
  • Common hippos can run up to 30kph (19mph), making them the fastest land animal of their size. For comparison, the average person can only run around 13kph and has a much slower acceleration.
  • Common hippos are the most dangerous large land animal in the world, causing an estimated 500 deaths each year in Africa.
  • Despite being semi-aquatic, hippos cannot truly swim, instead simply trotting along the riverbeds where they live and surfacing for breath every 3-5 minutes.
  • Hippos sweat out a natural sunscreen aptly named hipposudoric acid. This sweat is what gives hippos their reddish tint.
  • Despite their threatening appearance and statistics, hippos are true herbivores and only attack in percieved self defense.
  • Pygmy hippos are half of a common hippos size, as well as much less agressive and much more cute!
  • Pygmy hippos unfortunatly are quite endangered at the moment.Click the image below to find out more.
